✈️ NRI Voting Guide

Everything you need to know to vote from abroad.

Eligibility

Indian citizens living abroad who have NOT acquired foreign citizenship can register as overseas voters.

Registration

Fill Form 6A online on the ECI portal. You'll need your valid Indian Passport and visa details.

How to Vote

Currently, NRIs must travel to India to vote in person at their registered constituency. E-postal ballots are being considered but not fully implemented.
