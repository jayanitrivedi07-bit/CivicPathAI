✈️ NRI Voting Guide

Static flow guidance for overseas Indian citizens.

1

Eligibility Rules

Must be a citizen of India absent from place of ordinary residence in India owing to employment, education or otherwise. Must NOT have acquired citizenship of any other country.

2

Form 6A Process

Download or fill Form 6A online at the ECI portal. Attach self-attested copies of your valid passport pages containing your photo and valid visa.

3

Embassy Limitations

You cannot cast your vote at an embassy. Overseas electors must vote in person at their designated polling station in India.

4

Deadlines

Registration must be completed at least a few weeks prior to the finalization of the electoral roll. Check the live timeline for exact dates.
