# VoteSathi AI

VoteSathi AI is a simple, straightforward application designed to guide Indian citizens through the voting process. 
Whether you're a first-time voter, an NRI, or have recently moved, VoteSathi AI asks a few simple questions to determine your eligibility and tells you exactly what to do next to cast your vote.

## Features
- **Eligibility Checker**: Find out if you are eligible to vote and what documents you need based on your age and location.
- **Guidance for All**: Tailored advice for regular citizens, NRIs, and people who have recently moved.
- **Election Process Overview**: A simple step-by-step guide to how elections work in India.
- **FAQs**: Answers to common questions about the voting process.
- **No Data Collection**: Your privacy is respected. No personal data is stored or transmitted.

## Running Locally
Just open `index.html` in your browser. No build steps or local servers are required for this basic setup.

## Disclaimer
This tool is for guidance only. Information provided is based on Election Commission of India (ECI) guidelines. For the most accurate and up-to-date information, please visit [voters.eci.gov.in](https://voters.eci.gov.in) or call the Voter Helpline at **1950**.
